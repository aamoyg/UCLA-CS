Midterm exam issues

MATERIAL

The midterm includes material up to and including projections. The main  subjects that you have to know about are:

  • Vectors calculus, Matrix calculus, homogeneous coordinates
  • Line, plane representations (implicit, explicit, parametric).
  • Geometric transformations (2D and 3D)
    • Elementary transformations and their properties
    • Composition, Inversion, Change of basis
    • Rotation about an arbitrary point/axis
    • Changing  coordinate systems
    • Camera transformation
  • Projections (parallel, perspective, etc.)
    • Projection matrices
    • Projections of lines
    • View volumes
  • OpenGL programming
    • Geometric transformations in OpenGL
    • Matrix Stack.
    • Camera transformation, gluLookAt()
    • Projections in OpenGL

Subjects that have not been covered or are out of the scope of the midterm are:

  • 2D and 3D Clipping.
INSTRUCTIONS

The exam is closed book.

You may NOT use or have on your desk calculators and other electronic devices such as cellphones, pagers, PDAs etc.

You do not need to bring bluebooks. The instructor will provide xam sheets.

Duration:  It will be between 1h and 1h30minutes.

Good luck!
